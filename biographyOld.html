Tim Gauthier—Guitar  

I started playing guitar as a teenager in Lincoln Nebraska, and it has been my passion ever since. My musical taste is eclectic, and I draw from a varied background of musical experience. Bands I have played with include The Devil's Thumb Bluegrass Band, Touch Monkeys, Close to Home, Hugh Regan & Marc Sabatella, T-Band, Cowtown Boogie, and The Mongomery Jazz Group. I was the 2001 guitar champion at the Rockygrass Bluegrass Festival and have competed in the National Flat-Picking Championships in Winfield, Kansas. While completing my degree in music education at Colorado State University, I enjoyed playing in different jazz ensembles. For over twenty years, I have played as a member or side man in a variety of groups.  Playing jazz with Colorado front range musicians has helped me better understand this great American art form, given me a chance to challenge my level of musicianship, and provided me opportunities to meet a lot of really fun people.
