Tim Gauthier

Welcome to my website! It has truly been a pleasure playing jazz over the years with my friends in Colorado. This spontaneous music is always inspiring.

I have been fortunate to play with many talented folks in the area and am excited about what lies ahead. I perform with several groups based in Fort Collins, Colorado. I work frequently with a trio of guitar, bass, and drums and also take pleasure in performing solo.

I hope you enjoy browsing the site. I especially hope you like the sound and video clips included on the music page.
