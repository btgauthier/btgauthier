Welcome to my website!  It has truly been a pleasure playing different kinds of music over the years with my friends in Fort Collins , Colorado .  I have been fortunate to play with lots of very talented folks in the area and am excited about what lies ahead.  Over the past several years my musical efforts have been centered around jazz.  This spontaneous music continues to inspire me.  Recently, I have taken great pleasure in meeting new kindred spirits.  Aside from playing with the Montgomery Jazz Group, it has been fun working with a trio of guitar, bass, and drums. 

I hope you enjoy browsing the site.  I especially hope you like the trio sound clips included on the music page.  These samples feature trio performances with several area musicians.  Thanks to John Johnson , Chuck Landgraf , Scott Ford, Phil Spencer, Roger Barnhart, and Chris Dale for making the gigs fun and memorable.  I look forward to our next performance.
